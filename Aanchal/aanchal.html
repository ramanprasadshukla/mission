Aastha School, located in Jawanga Geedam, Dantewada, Chhattisgarh, is committed to providing students with excellent sports facilities to nurture their physical and mental well-being. The school boasts spacious playgrounds where children actively engage in games like football, volleyball, and traditional sports.

Students who are part of the school sports teams participate in regular morning practice sessions to improve their skills and fitness levels. Aastha School’s sports programs promote overall development and encourage teamwork, discipline, and a healthy lifestyle among all its students.
